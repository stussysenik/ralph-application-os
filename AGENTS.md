## Working Rules

- Stay spec-driven and TDD-driven.
- Treat the semantic kernel as the source of truth.
- Prefer internal builders over platform-specific shortcuts.
- Use block comments for module invariants and non-obvious logic.
- Update `PROGRESS.md` when repo reality changes.
- Do not add heavy infrastructure without a benchmark or proof obligation.

## Named Roles

- `researcher`
- `semantic-architect`
- `builder`
- `verifier`
- `promoter`

Promotion requires proof. No role gets to invent semantics and self-promote them without verification.
